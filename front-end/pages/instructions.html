TOBEDEFINED - FANTASY BASKETBALL LEAGUE MANAGER

INSTRUCTIONS

Fantasy basketball works on the same basic principles as any other fantasy sport: the game is based on constructing teams of players from the respective league and earning points based on their real-life performances.

Real life stats are worth a amount of fantasy points(FP). Points are worth 1 fantasy point, assists and rebounds are worth 2 fantasy points, and blocks and steals are worth 2.5 fantasy points.

Draft your team, insert your players, and as time goes on insert their games. You can check on the leaderboard who has the most points out of all users.

Each team can have up to 12 players, however you may only add 656 games per season, out of a total possible of 984. This is done so you're not screwd by real life injuries. You can also avoid inserting bad games in favor of better ones to some degree.

Good luck and have fun!
